FAQs and Answers

General

What is DecaSave?

DecaSave is a simple app that helps you save with ease.
You can choose to save little amounts of money periodically (Daily, Weekly or Monthly) towards a specific savings target for a specified period of time.
DecaSave helps you stop excessive spending by letting you easily keep funds away that you will not like to touch.
We take away the stress by combining simplicity, discipline, convenience, and flexibility to enable you to manage your finances better and achieve financial freedom.
